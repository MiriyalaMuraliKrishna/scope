    FAQs

    Do I need any coding knowledge before joining the course?

    No. Our programs are beginner-friendly and designed to help students with zero technical background learn step-by-step and build strong development fundamentals.

    What is the duration of the Full Stack and Full Stack + Gen AI courses?

    The duration ranges from 3-4 months depending on the program. Both include structured modules, hands-on projects, assessments, and interview preparation.

    Will I get placement support after completing the course?

    Yes. Scoop Labs provides dedicated placement assistance including resume building, mock interviews, portfolio development, and job referrals through our hiring network.

    Are the classes conducted online or offline?

    We offer classroom-based training in Banashankari, Bangalore, supported by mentor-led sessions, assignments, and real-time project practice.

    How many projects will I build during the course?

    Each learner builds multiple real-world projects, capstone applications, and GitHub-ready assignments that demonstrate practical skills to employers.

    What makes Scoop Labs different from other training institutes?

    Scoop Labs offers real industry mentorship, small batch learning, hands-on development, modern tools, NSDC alignment, and placement-focused training, ensuring every learner becomes truly job-ready.
